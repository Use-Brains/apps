## Hero section

- **Headline:** "Turn walkthrough notes, photos, and voice memos into a reviewable draft proposal package in minutes."
- **Subheadline:** "Built for small commercial cleaning companies. You review everything before it goes out."
- **CTA:** "Get Started" → sign-up/dashboard

## How it works (3–4 steps)

- Upload your walkthrough notes, photos, and voice memos
- AI extracts and organizes site details — surfaces what's confirmed, what's assumed, and what's missing
- Review and edit the structured summary
- Generate your scope, checklist, proposal draft, follow-up email, and internal questions

## What you get section

- Brief descriptions of the 5 outputs
- Use "draft" language throughout
- Emphasize that outputs are reviewable first-pass materials, not final documents

## Trust signals

- "All outputs are drafts — you review before anything goes out"
- "Better to be incomplete than falsely specific"
- "Your data stays private to your company"
- "Built for 2–25 person cleaning companies doing recurring office and commercial work"

## Messaging rules

### ✅ ALLOWED

- "Saves admin time"
- "Turns messy inputs into professional drafts"
- "Improves consistency"
- "Highlights missing info"
- "Works with your existing workflow"

### ❌ NOT ALLOWED

- "Automatic quoting"
- "Accurate pricing"
- "Guaranteed win rates"
- "AI janitorial software"
- "Bidding automation"
- "Compliance-safe"

## Responsive requirements

- Landing page must work cleanly on desktop, tablet, and mobile portrait
- No horizontal overflow
- Text must remain readable without zoom
- CTA and navigation must be easy to tap on mobile
- Sections must stack cleanly on narrow screens
- Avoid dense layouts that only work on desktop
