## Dependencies

- Run **after** pilot-readiness task (#440935) completes.
- Run **after** mobile usability task (#465631) completes.

The product must be safe, demoable, trust-ready, and usable on common devices before real users touch it.

---

## Ideal pilot profile (say yes to)

- Owner-led commercial cleaning company
- 2–25 cleaners
- Custom walkthrough-based quoting process
- Recurring office / professional office / standard small commercial work
- Lightly staffed back office
- Already using phone notes, voice memos, photos, and templates for bids
- Not expecting full pricing automation or a complete operating system
- Willing to review draft outputs manually and give honest feedback

## Do not target yet

- Complex medical clinics
- Schools or government facilities
- Large janitorial firms (50+ employees)
- Facilities with heavy compliance expectations
- Companies demanding automatic pricing from day one
- Anyone who needs PDF/DOCX export, scheduling, CRM, or deeper workflow integrations to adopt

---

## What to do

- Find 10 cleaning companies matching the pilot profile
- Use Google Maps, local directories, LinkedIn, chamber listings
- Prioritize direct sourcing over community channels
- Select the 5 strongest fits for initial outreach
- Craft personalized outreach from mintapps@polsia.app
- Founder-to-founder, not sales pitch
- Frame as: _"We built a walkthrough-to-bid copilot that turns notes, photos, and voice memos into a reviewable draft proposal package. We're looking for a few small commercial cleaning companies to test it and tell us whether it actually saves time."_
- **Offer:** free pilot access, concierge onboarding, direct founder support, feedback calls
- **Ask:** test with one real walkthrough + a 15-minute feedback call afterward
- Keep under 150 words
- Be honest about product maturity and review-required workflow
- Send 5 personalized emails
- Log all contacts, ranked by fit and response potential

### Pre-send check

- Confirm the live app is usable enough on desktop and mobile/tablet for first pilot impressions
- Use the current demo account/sample walkthrough as the basis for accurate messaging and screenshots only if needed

### Messaging constraints (do **not** say)

- ❌ "AI-powered quoting"
- ❌ "Accurate estimates"
- ❌ "Automated bidding"
- ❌ "Janitorial software"
- ❌ "Ready-to-send proposals"
- ❌ "Works fully on mobile" unless the mobile task has actually resolved the core usability issues

### Messaging constraints (do say)

- ✅ "Walkthrough-to-bid copilot"
- ✅ "Turns messy notes into draft proposals"
- ✅ "Surfaces missing info before you send"
- ✅ "You review everything before it goes out"
- ✅ "Built for 2–25 person commercial cleaning companies"

### Success metric

- 5 personalized outreach emails sent to high-fit prospects
- All contacts logged and ranked by fit/response potential
- Outreach quality and pilot fit matter more than reply count
