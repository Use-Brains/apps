## 4. Description

AI Notecards helps you turn source material into study decks faster.

Create flashcards from text, notes, and photos. Study with multiple modes. Explore curated decks from the marketplace. Keep learning moving whether you are reviewing your own material or discovering a deck that is already ready to study.

CREATE DECKS FASTER
Paste text, type a topic, or use photos of notes to generate flashcards. Review the cards, save the deck, and keep studying without building everything by hand.

STUDY YOUR WAY
Use multiple study modes to review cards in different ways and keep sessions from getting repetitive.

EXPLORE CURATED DECKS
Browse marketplace categories, open deck detail pages, and evaluate decks by title, description, pricing, ratings, and seller information.

BUILT FOR SERIOUS STUDY
AI Notecards is designed around repeat study, not just one-time generation. Create decks, revisit them, and keep your study library organized in one place.

USEFUL FOR
- exam prep
- class review
- language study
- certification study
- self-directed learning

## 8. Review note guidance

Use reviewer notes that match the actual launch build.

If Apple subscriptions are active in the build:

- tell reviewers where subscription options appear
- tell reviewers where Restore Purchases appears
- explain how existing entitlement is refreshed

If marketplace deck purchasing is not enabled in-app at launch:

- do not imply that reviewers can buy marketplace decks inside the app
- describe the marketplace as a curated discovery and deck-access surface for the current release

If marketplace deck purchasing is enabled:

- explain exactly how it works in the build under review
- make sure the note matches the product behavior and your legal/compliance stance
