Report Date: March 21, 2026

Status: Ready for implementation

Executive Summary

Key Strategic Findings:

Title field is highest-impact: Apple's algorithm heavily weights keywords in the app title (30 chars max). Include 1-2 primary keywords after brand name.

Knowt owns "free flashcards": You can't beat their positioning, but you can own "marketplace" + "creator payouts" angle.

Massive untapped gap: No competitor targets "buy study decks" or "study marketplace" keywords. This is YOUR competitive moat.

Spaced repetition + AI is viable: High volume, moderate competition. Flashka/Knowt partially own it, but you can differentiate with "free" + "marketplace."

"Study from photos": Very low competition, high intent. Unique to ai-notecards' photo upload + AI generation feature.

Recommendation: Position as "Free AI Flashcard Maker & Marketplace" — emphasizes free tier, AI generation, AND the creator monetization differentiation.

Quizlet: "study," "flashcards," "learn," "quiz," "test" — owned

Knowt: "free flashcards," "learn mode," "AI," "study guides" — owned

Anki: "spaced repetition," "flashcard software" — owned

Flashka: "AI flashcards," "professor," "quiz" — owned

Gaps: Opportunities You Should OWN

Gap Why It Matters Your Advantage
"study marketplace" No one ranks for this. It's a literal feature gap in the market. First-mover advantage. Your unique differentiator.
"buy study decks" Search volume exists, but supply is weak (Quizlet's marketplace is dead). You're launching with curated decks. Own this keyword.
"study from photos" AI + camera = trending. Competitors mention it passively. You're leading with this feature. Make it a keyword.
"study streaks" Gamification trend. Duolingo owns this, but no flashcard app owns it. You have this. Own it.
"free AI" AI tools increasingly paywalled. Free + AI = rare combo. Position hard. "Free AI flashcards" is underserved.
"create flashcards" Very high volume but everyone targets it. Different angle: "create from photos" or "create from notes." Your photo + AI angle is unique. Differentiate within this keyword space.
"sell study decks" Creator economy keyword. No education app targets this. You're the first marketplace for creators.

Keywords to Avoid (Waste of Chars)

"Quizlet" — Apple penalizes competitor keywords

"study guide" — Knowt owns this with AI feature

"cramming" — negative connotation; low volume

"homework" — too broad; overlaps with study

"tutoring" — different category; confuses ASO

8. Key ASO Rules for Launch

DO:

✅ Include 1-2 high-volume keywords in title

✅ Make subtitle clear value proposition ("Make & Buy")

✅ Update description with long-tail keywords naturally (not keyword-stuffed)

✅ Screenshot #1 should show your differentiator (photo → AI generation)

✅ Respond to user reviews ASAP (Apple indexes review keywords)

✅ Get 4.5+ rating before aggressive ASO push

DON'T:

❌ Keyword stuff (comma-separated list in description looks spammy)

❌ Use competitor names (Apple penalizes)

❌ Change name/keywords too frequently (takes 2-4 weeks to re-index)

❌ Use non-English keywords if targeting US market

❌ Claim you have features you don't (Flashka's "AI tutor" backlash = bad reviews)
